Welcome to FZEE Flasher(beta)! The -UNOFFICAL- Flipper Zero ESP32 Dev board web flasher.

This website is designed to assist you in updating the firmware of your ESP32 Dev Boards for the Flipper Zero. To utilize this web tool, follow these steps:

  1. Check if your ESP32 Dev Board needs to be in bootloader mode. This is done by holding boot button and pluging in the USB at the same time. (some boards may not require this step.)
  2. Click on the "Connect" button located at the top right of the page. A dialog box will appear, prompting you to connect the paired COM device. Click "Connect."
  3. In the "Select Model" menu that appears, use the drop-down menus to choose your (board), (version), and (firmware).
  4. Click on "Program." We've included an (erase) function in case you encounter issues with your ESP32.
  5. Once the flashing process is complete, your ESP32 board will be ready to use with its new firmware.

FZEE Flasher FAQ:

Q: Is FZEE Flasher affiliated with Flipper Devices or any other custom hardware sites?
A: No, this site was built for the community by the community.

Q: Why can I not connect my ESP32 and get the error browser not supported?
A: Some Browsers have disabled serial support (for security) and will not allow a connection to the ESP32 board. You will need to enable it in the browser or use a browser that supports it.

Q: Will you support X firmware and when?
A: FZEE Flasher is very new and in beta. We do intend to support many poplar firmware's in the near future including ones from some 3rd party hardware venders.

Q: How can I help FZEE Flasher?
A: We are planning to open the site up to the community for further development in the near future.
