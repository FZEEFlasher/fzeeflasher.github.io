Welcome to FZEE Flasher (beta)! The UNOFFICIAL Flipper Zero ESP32 Dev board web flasher.

This website is designed to assist you in updating the firmware of your ESP32 Dev Boards for the Flipper Zero. To utilize this web tool, follow these steps:

  1. You might need to install the driver from Silicon Labs in order for the ESP32 to be recognized.
  2. Check if your ESP32 Dev Board needs to be in bootloader mode. This is done by holding the boot button and plugging in the USB at the same time. (some boards may not require this step.)
  3. Click on the "Connect" button located at the top right of the page. A dialog box will appear, prompting you to connect the paired COM device. Click "Connect."
  4. In the "Select Model" menu that appears, use the drop-down menus to choose your board, version, and firmware.
  5. Click on "Program." We've included an erase function in case you encounter issues with your ESP32.
  6. Once the flashing process is complete, your ESP32 board will be ready to use with its new firmware.

    8. * If your ESP32 is getting an connection error try to reboot or power cycle the dev board and refresh the web-page.

FZEE Flasher FAQ:

Q: Is FZEE Flasher affiliated with Flipper Devices or any other custom hardware sites?

A: No, this site was built for the community by the community.


Q: Why can I not connect my ESP32 and get the error "browser not supported"?

A: Some browsers have disabled serial support for security reasons and will not allow a connection to the ESP32 board. You will need to enable it in the browser or use a browser that supports it.


Q: Will you support X firmware and when?

A: FZEE Flasher is very new and in beta. We do intend to support many popular firmwares in the near future, including those from some 3rd party hardware vendors.


Q: How can I help FZEE Flasher?

A: We are planning to open the site up to the community for further development in the near future.
